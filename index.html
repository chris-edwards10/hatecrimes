New York City Hate Crimes outpacing last year

By: Christopher Edwards - Nov. 14, 2024

Hate Crimes in New York City are already outpacing those reported by this time last year, according to NYPD data. By Sep. 30 2024, there were 519 reported hate crimes in NYC, compared to 478 by the same date last year, an 8.5% increase. Hate Crimes overall have been on the rise since 2020. They rose 16.2% from 2021 too 2022, and 12.3% from 2022 to 2023.

Based on this trend, total hate crimes for this year could exceed last year's number of 755.
